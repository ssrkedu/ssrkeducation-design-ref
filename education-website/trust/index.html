About SSRK Educational Trust

Building Odisha's Future,
One Student at a Time

Founded in 1988 by a group of visionary educators committed to uplifting Odisha through quality higher education, SSRK Educational Trust has grown from a single degree college into a comprehensive group of six institutions spanning arts, science, engineering, pharmacy, education, and management.

Rooted in Odia values and guided by the belief that education is the most powerful tool for social transformation, SSRK Trust serves students from rural and urban backgrounds alike — offering world-class facilities at accessible costs.
