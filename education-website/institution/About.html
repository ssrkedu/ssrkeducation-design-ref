Our Story

Built on a Vision of Accessible Education

SSRK Degree College was established in 1990 under the SSRK Educational Trust with a singular mission — to make quality higher education accessible to students across Odisha, especially those from rural and semi-urban backgrounds who had limited choices.

Beginning with just three programmes and a small batch of students, the college has grown into one of the most trusted undergraduate institutions in Cuttack, offering over 12 programmes across Arts, Science, Commerce and Pharmacy — all with government approval and university affiliation.

Today, more than 4,000 students study on our campus each year, supported by 80+ faculty members, modern laboratories, a rich library, and a culture of academic integrity and personal growth.

"Education is not just about degrees — it is about building the capacity to think, to lead, and to contribute meaningfully to society. At SSRK, we have always believed that every deserving student, regardless of background, must have access to quality education."

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted undergraduate institution in Odisha — known not just for academic excellence, but for producing graduates who are ethical, confident, and ready to contribute to society and the economy.

To provide affordable, government-approved education with caring faculty, modern facilities, and student support systems — so every student who walks through our doors has the tools and confidence to succeed.
