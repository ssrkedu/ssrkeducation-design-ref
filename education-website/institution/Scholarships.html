How to Apply

1
Take Admission
Complete your admission at SSRK Degree College and receive your student ID and admission receipt.
2
Visit Scholarship Cell
Visit the Scholarship Cell (Room 104, Admin Block) within 30 days of admission with original documents.
3
Submit Documents
Submit caste certificate, income certificate, +2 marksheet, bank passbook copy, Aadhaar, and two passport photos.

5
Verification & Disbursement
After document verification, scholarship amount is credited directly to your bank account within 60–90 days.
FAQs

Common Questions

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

Yes. You can apply for both a government scholarship (ST/SC, OBC) and the institution's merit scholarship. However, only the higher-value scheme will be applied to your fee account.

When is the scholarship deadline?

Government scholarship applications (NSP portal) typically close in November–December each year. Institution merit scholarships must be applied within 30 days of admission.

Is the merit scholarship renewable?

Yes. The merit scholarship is renewed each year if the student maintains 60% or above in their annual university examination.

What documents do I need?

You need: Aadhaar card, caste/category certificate, income certificate, Class 10 & 12 marksheets, bank passbook (student name), admission fee receipt, and two passport-size photographs.

I need help filling the NSP form. Who do I contact?

Our Scholarship Cell staff assist students free of charge every working day (Mon–Sat, 10 AM–4 PM) in Room 104, Admin Block. You can also call +91 674 255 0001.
