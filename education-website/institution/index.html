About the College

Quality Education for Every Student

SSRK Degree College has been shaping the futures of students across Odisha since 1990. Affiliated to Utkal University and approved by the Government of Odisha, our campus provides a nurturing environment where academic excellence meets holistic development.

Our faculty bring decades of teaching experience, research contributions, and industry connections — giving students both the theoretical foundation and practical exposure needed for modern careers.

Whether you are a student from Cuttack, a parent exploring options for your child, or a teacher looking to grow — SSRK Degree College is committed to your success at every step.
