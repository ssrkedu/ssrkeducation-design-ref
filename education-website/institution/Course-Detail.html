Diploma in Pharmacy

2 Years
60 Seats
PCI Approved

The Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) is a two-year full-time programme approved by the Pharmacy Council of India and affiliated to the State Board of Technical Education, Odisha. It provides students with a strong foundation in pharmaceutical sciences, preparing them for careers across hospitals, retail pharmacies, and the pharmaceutical industry.

What You Will Learn

Pharmaceutical Chemistry and Drug Analysis
Pharmacognosy and Pharmacology
Hospital and Clinical Pharmacy
Drug Store Management and Regulations

Eligibility Criteria

Passed Class 12 (10+2) with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects
Mathematics or Biology as optional subject
Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST candidates)
Minimum age: 17 years as on date of admission

Documents Required

Class 10 and 12 marksheets and certificates
Aadhaar card, caste certificate (if applicable)
4 passport-size photographs, migration certificate

Year 1 — Core Subjects

Pharmaceutics — I
Pharmaceutical Chemistry — I
Pharmacognosy
Biochemistry and Clinical Pathology
Human Anatomy and Physiology

Year 2 — Advanced Subjects

Pharmaceutics — II
Pharmaceutical Chemistry — II
Pharmacology and Toxicology
Hospital and Clinical Pharmacy
Drug Store and Business Management

Career Opportunities

D.Pharm graduates have excellent career prospects across multiple sectors of the healthcare and pharmaceutical industry.

Registered Pharmacist at hospitals and clinics
Own a retail pharmacy / medical store
Drug Inspector / Government Pharmacy services
Medical Representative / Pharma Sales
Pharmaceutical manufacturing companies
Further study: B.Pharm, M.Pharm
